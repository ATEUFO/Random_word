# Guessing games

*This application is a mini guessing game.*

The goal is to guess the word chosen by the computer. The word list is limited and may be updated.
This game is in French and no other language version has been developed.

## Rules

The player sees the underscores *_ _ _* corresponding to the length of the word. They must choose a letter. If they find the letter, the underscore changes to the chosen letter until they find the complete word.

`But be careful, attempts are limited.`

Example:
the word is **hello**
```
> _ _ _ _ _

> Enter a latter: e

> _ e _ _ _

> Enter a latter: o

> _ e _ _ o

> Enter a latter: l

> _ e l l o

> Enter a latter: i

> _ e l l o

> Enter a latter: h

> h e l l o

> you win ... 

```
